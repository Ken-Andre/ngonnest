# Product Context - NgoNest

## Why NgoNest Exists

### The Problem
In Cameroon, household management is traditionally done through memory, paper notes, and verbal communication. This leads to:
- **Significant food waste** (estimated 30-40% of household budget)
- **Budget overruns** from forgotten purchases and impulse buying
- **Stress** from uncertainty about household stock levels
- **Time waste** from frequent market trips for forgotten items
- **Poor planning** for family events, holidays, and seasonal needs

### The Solution
NgoNest transforms household management into a simple, visual, and predictive experience that feels familiar to WhatsApp users while providing powerful insights.

## How It Should Work

### Core User Journey
1. **Setup (5 minutes)**: User creates household profile with basic information
2. **Daily Use (2 minutes)**: Quick inventory checks and consumption logging
3. **Weekly Planning (5 minutes)**: Review predictions and plan purchases
4. **Monthly Review (10 minutes)**: Analyze budget and adjust patterns

### Key Features by User Value

#### Inventory Management
- **Visual categorization** using familiar Cameroonian product groupings
- **Smart search** that understands local product names and variations
- **Quantity tracking** with visual indicators (full, half, low, empty)
- **Expiration alerts** with local market context
- **Offline-first** with optional cloud sync

#### Budget Tracking
- **Visual spending analysis** with charts familiar to mobile money users
- **Category-based budgets** aligned with Cameroonian spending patterns
- **Predictive alerts** before budget limits are reached
- **Export to Excel** for tax or accounting purposes

#### Smart Predictions
- **Consumption patterns** based on household size and preferences
- **Seasonal adjustments** for holidays, fasting periods, and celebrations
- **Price tracking** with local market variations
- **Waste prevention** through timely consumption alerts

## User Experience Goals

### Simplicity First
- **No complex menus**: Everything accessible in 3 taps maximum
- **Visual feedback**: Progress bars, color coding, and familiar icons
- **Contextual help**: Tooltips and hints that appear when needed
- **Forgiving interface**: Easy undo actions and clear error recovery

### Cultural Adaptation
- **Language options**: French, English, and major local languages
- **Local pricing**: Integration with Cameroonian market prices
- **Cultural events**: Awareness of holidays, fasting periods, and celebrations
- **Social features**: Family sharing and community tips (optional)

### Accessibility Standards
- **Large touch targets**: Minimum 44px for all interactive elements
- **High contrast**: WCAG AA compliance (4.5:1 contrast ratio)
- **Clear typography**: Minimum 16px font size for readability
- **Screen reader support**: Full accessibility for visually impaired users
